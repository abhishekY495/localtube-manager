LocalTube Manager

Thank you for installing LTM extension. Let's pin the extension and set a shortcut for easy access.

  1. 1 Click the  puzzle piece icon  in the top-right corner of your Chrome browser.
  2. 2 Find  LocalTube Manager  in the dropdown list.
  3. 3 Click the  pin icon  next to the extension name.

That's it! Your extension is now pinned and ready to use.
